Manual Audio Transcription

The General Setup

You write on the raspberry pi with a keyboard connecdted to that. This way you do not need any special software to handle focus. You plug in the keyboard to the raspberry pi and connect to it via ssh or vnc. On the other computer you need to setup the foot pedal. SMplayer seems a good choice for playback as it is available on any system. It is a GUI version of mplayer and supports jumping back and adjusting playback speed.

On Mac

  • Enable the Foot Pedal. Is is recognized? lsusb
  • get the App Enjoyable
  • map arrow keys to the different buttons on the pedal
  • make sure tha Enjoyable is enabled in the status bar
  • download smplayer.

  • Togle Play SPACE Button 2 on Footcontrol
  • Increase playback Speed RICHT ARROW Button 1 on Foot Control
  • Decrease payback speed LEFT ARROW Button 3 on Foot Control
  • Jump back 5 seconds DOWN ARROW Button 4 on Foot Control
