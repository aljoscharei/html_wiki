Combat Speciality

At 6th level, your intense dedication to your weapon training shows itself with a new feature:
- For Sword Saints, you can use your reaction after taking damage from a melee attack to make a single attack roll against that attacker with your sword if the attacker is in reach. - For Axe Artists, when you succeed at your first attack roll you can use your reaction to gain advantage on your second attack roll. - For Spear Dancers as long as you wield only your spear, you have a +1 bonus to AC. - For Flailers, when an attacker fails on their attack roll, you can use your reaction to ensnare their weapon. Make an opposed Strength check, if you succeed the attacker is disarmed.
