Underwater Combat

When adventurers pursue sahuagin back to their undersea homes, fight off sharks in an ancient shipwreck, or find themselves in a flooded dungeon room, they must fight in a challenging environment. Underwater the following rules apply. Underwater melee Underwater ranged attack

Creatures and objects that are fully immersed in water have resistance to fire damage.
