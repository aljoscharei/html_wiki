Dagger of the murderer

Schaden 1d4. Kill a cultural humanoid being in a 10 minute sacrificial ritual.

The Blood of the Sacrifice sticks to the blade until an Attack using this weapon causes at least one point of damage to a creature.

A being hit by this weapon dies instantly: - if it suffers at least one point of damage from this weapon AND - if it currently has 100 hit points or fewer.
