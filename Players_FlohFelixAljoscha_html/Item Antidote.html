Item Antidote

Weak DC 5 plus 2 5 Th 2d6 Moderate DC 10 plus 5 10 Th 2d4 Strong DC 15 plus 10 15 Th d4 This elixir provides a bonus to the next saving throw within 24 hours that a character makes against a poison effect. While it helps stop the poison, it does not cure any damage the creature has already sustained due to the poison. For each level the DC to create the elixir and the bonus to the saving throw are given.
