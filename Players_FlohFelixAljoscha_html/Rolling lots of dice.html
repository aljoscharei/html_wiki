Rolling lots of dice

The following tables help you roll lots of checks using only a single d20. This adds extra randomness to the “one quarter succeed” guideline outlined in the “202108260433 Mass Combat in RPG Handlings Mobs Running Hordes MOC” chapter of the Lazy DM’s Companion. To use the table, follow the following steps:

  • Determine how many rolls to abstract: 5, 10, or 20, and consult that table.

  • Determine the target number for the roll by either subtracting the attackers attack bonus from the defender’s AC or the defender’s saving throw bonus from the attacker’s DC.

  • Find the target number on the top row of the table.

  • Roll 1d20 and look down the column to see which row matches your roll.

  • Look to the outside “Successes” columns to see how

    many rolls succeed.
