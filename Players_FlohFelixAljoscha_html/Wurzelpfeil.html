Root Arrow

Each Arrow Shot causes 1 temporary Corruption. A Target hit by this Arrow takes 1d8 additional damage and is Restrained by roots that grow from the Arrow through the Target in to the ground. The Arrow no effect on creatures that are formless. A creature can use its action to make a Strength check agains DC 15 or against the Spell Save DC of the marksman, whichever is higher, freeing itself or another creature within its reach on a success.

Whenever a root arrow is shot, it is lost because it will grow roots and will gro into a Tree, regardless of whether it hit a target or not.
