Ruins Extended Rest 24h

An extended rest requires at least (24 hours) in a safe place where you can sleep, relax and tend to your wounds without threat of interruption. Extended rests often mark the end of an adventure, or at least a significant break in the action. You gain the following benefits at the end of an extendedrest: - You regain all of your hit points.
- You recover all of your Hit Dice.
- Your temporary Corruption becomes 0.
